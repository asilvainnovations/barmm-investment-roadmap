Balanced Scorecard Investment Roadmap (2026–2035)

×

1. Limits to Growth

Investment growth potential faces limiting factors including infrastructure deficits, skills gaps, and governance capacity constraints.

💡 Key Insight: The Growth Ceiling

Initial growth produced by reinforcing processes eventually slows due to balancing forces. Without proactive investment in enabling infrastructure, growth plateaus as the system encounters resource constraints.

🎯 Strategic Response

LP2 Infrastructure-Energy-Connectivity Nexus: Invest in energy infrastructure (80-100% electrification by 2028) and digital connectivity (70% internet penetration by 2030) to raise the growth ceiling before momentum stalls.

×

2. Success to the Successful

Early success in the halal industry could divert disproportionate resources, creating imbalances and neglecting other priority sectors such as tourism, agribusiness, and renewable energy.

💡 Key Insight: Resource Imbalance

Initial success creates a virtuous cycle for the winning sector but a vicious cycle for others. Without intervention, halal industry growth could starve tourism, agribusiness, and renewable energy of necessary investment.

🎯 Strategic Response

Integrated Halal-Driven Strategy: Establish sectoral balance criteria and periodic resource reallocation reviews. Ensure halal industry development includes value chain integration with agriculture and supports cross-sectoral infrastructure.

×

3. Fixes that Fail

Ad hoc tax incentives without systemic governance reform may attract some initial investment but fail to build the institutional capacity needed for sustained investment attraction.

💡 Key Insight: The Symptom Relief Trap

Quick fixes provide temporary relief but create unintended consequences (revenue loss, institutional weakness) that worsen the fundamental problem over time. The "fix" becomes the addiction.

🎯 Strategic Response

LP3 Governance-Investor Confidence: Address root causes through systemic governance reforms rather than temporary patches. Implement transparent regulatory frameworks, streamline BBNR to 1-day registration, and establish international accreditation for BHB.
