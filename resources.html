Analytical Frameworks

Key Frameworks Used in This Workshop

Four complementary analytical frameworks are woven through every session, each selected for its capacity to reveal systemic interconnections in Bangsamoro's investment landscape.

🌐

PEDSET Analysis

External Environmental Scanning

A six-domain macro-environmental framework examining the forces that shape Bangsamoro's investment climate. Used in Session 1 to identify external opportunities and systemic threats.

Political — governance autonomy & transition
Economic — GRDP performance & trade balance
Demographics — population youth dividend
Social — Islamic heritage & peacebuilding
Environmental — climate resilience & energy
Technological — digital transformation gaps
⚙️

McKinsey 7S Framework

Internal Institutional Assessment

An organisational analysis tool examining seven interdependent elements of Bangsamoro's governance system to surface internal alignment gaps and institutional strengths.

Strategy — BDP 2023-2028 & sectoral roadmaps
Structure — parliamentary system & 15 ministries
Systems — BBNR, BPLS, BEGMP platforms
Shared Values — Moral Governance framework
Skills — halal & digital expertise gaps
Staff — MILF-to-government transition
Style — collective decision-making culture
🔁

Systems Thinking & CLDs

Dynamic SWOT & Masterclass

Causal Loop Diagrams map circular cause-and-effect relationships to reveal reinforcing virtuous cycles and balancing constraints. Four feedback loops are identified as critical leverage points.

R1 — Investment-Development Virtuous Cycle
R2 — Governance-Investor Confidence Loop
B1 — Growth-Resource Constraints
B2 — Security-Investment Tensions
Archetypes: Limits to Growth, Fixes that Fail, Success to the Successful
🎯

Balanced Scorecard

Sessions 3 & 4 · KPI & Roadmap

Translates the Integrated Halal-Driven Investment Strategy into measurable objectives across four perspectives, operationalising the 2026–2035 roadmap with clear milestones and phase budgets.

Financial — PHP 50B investment target by 2035
Stakeholder — 2,000 halal-certified MSMEs
Internal Process — 1-day business registration
Learning & Growth — 100 halal officers trained
3-Phase: Foundation / Acceleration / Consolidation
